Bentota.
Turtle Hatchery

The Bentota Turtle Hatchery Project is a popular attraction in Bentota for all travellers. The pristine golden shores that draws you to our island, also provides an enticing nesting ground for several endangered turtle species from the Olive Ridley to the Leatherback turtle. The project aims at conserving and protecting the eggs from predators and other dangers. Visit the turtle hatchery in Bentota to learn about these endangered graceful creatures of the sea

GLASS BOTTOM BOAT RIDES


Glass Bottom Boat Rides are one of the most popular things to do in Bentota for individuals of any age, as it is safe and a completely ‘dry’ way to explore the colourful reefs and colourful marine life that thrives beneath the ocean’s surface. Whether your kids are too small to snorkel or you’re not a strong swimmer, these boat rides are the best way to spend the day while on the beach.

CORAL REEF EXPLORING


One thing that should never be overlooked when in Bentota are the stunning coral reefs. Discover a world of wonder that flourishes below the ocean’s surface on your snorkelling adventures in Bentota. With its thriving reef system located close to the shores, you’ll witness a myriad of marine life, including sea turtles! Explorers who carry PADI licenses are encouraged to partake in a scuba diving excursion with the experts as there are treasures that are waiting to be explored just off the coast of Sri Lanka, from shipwrecks to exotic marine life

SNORKLING WITH TURTLES


Take a break from your exploration around Sri Lanka and enjoy its beautiful waters when you join this snorkeling activity at Bentota! Take delight in Sri Lanka’s south coast and swim and snorkel to your heart’s content at the beach town of Bentota where you’ll get a chance to see green turtles in the wild! Bring your own action camera and take as many photos as you want as you swim alongside adorable turtles! No need to worry about bringing your own gear as well, as the operator will be there to assist you in all the items that you need. Just bring an excited heart and savor this unique experience in Bentota.
